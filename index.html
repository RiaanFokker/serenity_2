Serenity @ 14 Keurbooms River Lodge

Serenity is a self-catering apartment situated on the banks of the Keurbooms River in the beloved Keurbooms River Lodge. The apartment can sleep 4 people comfortably and is equipped with everything that you need for your holiday stay. Just a 10 minute walk from the ocean and close to many restaurants and activities along the Garden Route.
